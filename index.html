Upcoming Shows

Justin H. Min comes this Friday!

CUPB is exciting to cosponsor Cornell’s Asian Pacific Student Union (CAPSU) in presenting “An Evening with Cornell Alum Justin H. Min” on Friday, October 30 at 7pm EDT. Join CAPSU on Halloween Eve to learn about Cornell Alum Justin H. Min’s experience on Netflix’s The Umbrella Academy and Wong Fu Production’s Series Dating After College along with his time at Cornell and his thoughts on Asian American representation in the media. There will be a live audience Q&A along with a raffle for some signed posters!
