Upcoming Shows

CUPB and MCFAB are excited to present “Get Ready with Bob the Drag Queen”, season 8 winner of “RuPaul’s Drag Race” for a comedic conversation about his life, comedy, and career while doing a drag make up tutorial! Bob the Drag Queen will be coming virtually to Cornell on Friday, October 2nd 8pm. Sign up now!

As the season 8 winner of “RuPaul’s Drag Race,” Bob won three challenges including the Snatch Game. Bob is currently starring alongside Shangela Laquifa Wadley and Eureka O’Hara in HBO’s first unscripted hit series, “We’re Here.” Bob is also a cohost of Sibling Rivalry along with Monét X Change and has two comedy specials under his belt - the newest being Bob The Drag Queen: Live at Caroline’s. The moderator will be Matteo Lane, a New York based comedian who has appeared on Stephan Colbert, Late Night with Seth Meyers, HBO’s Crashing and WILL & GRACE.
