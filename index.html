Join Us

The Cornell University Program Board has weekly general body meetings (Wednesday at 5pm) during which we discuss speakers and comedians we'd like to see at Cornell as well as general pop culture. In non-COVID times we have snacks and meet in person in the Willard Straight Hall International Lounge. For now, we are meeting over Zoom.

To join CUPB you can sign up on our Slack where we post the weekly Zoom meeting links for our Wednesday 5pm meetings.

Sign up here to join our listserv with all meeting links!
